Spaghetti Recipe

Description

A very simple and easy recipe for beginner chefs learning how to cook on their own. Prepared with only the most simple and basic ingredients, this dish will be an absolute favorite on days when you're looking for a quick and easy meal. Absolutely delicious too, this one's for your local pasta and tomato lover. For vegans and vegetarians, the meat is optional

Ingredients

    spaghetti pasta
    tomato sauce, made or store-bought
    basil leaves
    onions
    ground meat of preference

Steps

    bring water in a pot to boil, and cook pasta until desired time written on package
    dice the onions
    in a pan with cooking oil, stir-fry the onions and ground meat of preference
    add tomato sauce and basil leaves, mix and cook until sauce simmers
    add cooked noodles to pan and mix with sauce
